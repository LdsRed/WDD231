Overview

Purpose

The Linux blog was created with the purpose of sharing good news about open source, free software, and upcoming events in the linux communities. Here you can find information about the latest releases of your favorite linux distributions and what they are implementing to stay relevant.

Audience

It is aimed at anyone who likes technology, software and linux in general.

Scenarios

What are the upcoming events from the linux community?

Where I can find the latest releases of my favorite linux distribution?

What is the best linux distribution for beginners?

What are the open source software alternatives I have available?
